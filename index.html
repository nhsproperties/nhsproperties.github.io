About NHS

NHS Property Management is a family business founded on a passion to share our expertise in property management and quality assurance. With a team based in Mississauga, and 3+ years of property management across the GTA, our property management ranges from managing individual condo units, to town houses, to single family homes. We established NHS to act an extension of our management experience so home owners and tenants can benefit from our services.

 

On behalf of property owners, we screen and match tenants to your requirements. We also help maintain your property.

 

On behalf of tenants, we ensure the highest quality of home living we can.

 

Our team is dedicated to giving you peace of mind 24/7.

Our Vision

To lead the single and family property management industry through a unique mix of innovative and tried-and-true methods.

Our Business Philosophy

Professionalism and efficiency

Our team dedicates its time and experience to provide all our clients with the highest quality property management we can.

 

Transparency and credibility

We run thorough screening and credit history checks on all prospective tenants to ensure that your property and tenant are a perfect match.

 

Thoroughness and attentiveness

Our maintenance and repair team of licensed technicians are available at the touch of a button. We perform routine visits to ensure that the property is well-maintained and is in its original state at the end of the leasing period, as it was at the start.

 

Communication

We are available 24/7 for emergency property management calls. Our foundation rests on consistent communication; we listen to your voice to respond to your needs, to ensure that both you and your tenant are satisfied.

Our Mission

We strive to turn your house into a home. NHS is Underpinned by high quality and cost effectiveness, and is dedicated to truly connecting homeowners and tenant.

Services

  • Move-in/out coordination
  • Periodic inspections
  • Coordinate move-in and move-out inspections
  • Coordinate minor and major repairs with handymen/contractors
  • Monthly rental collection and deposit for owner’s account after paying the bills

  • Inspection of property every 3 months
  • Email quotes for all work orders
  • Provide and collect keys upon departure
  • Mail forwarding
  • Interacting with tenant and city officials on owner’s behalf
  • Eviction support for problematic tenants‡

Lease/Rental

  • Advertising properties for lease
  • Coordinating all viewings and showing appointments
  • Applicant Screenings
  • Selecting the right tenant for you by carefully screening all applicants with: personal interview, employment verification, credit check, & landlord references

Building Rehabilitation/ Renovations

  • Upgrade and rehabilitation of aging properties of all kinds of single, multi-family and commercial units
  • Renovation of residential and commercial buildings
  • Hiring and coordination of specialized contractors for rehabilitation and renovation
